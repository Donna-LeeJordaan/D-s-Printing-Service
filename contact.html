Frequently Asked Questions

What are your turnaround times?

Standard orders are completed within 2-3 business days. Rush services are available for same-day or next-day completion (additional fees apply).

Do you offer design services?

Yes! We have in-house designers who can help create or modify your designs. Design services start at R150.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, JPG, and PNG files. For best results, we recommend PDF with bleed marks and CMYK color mode.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Absolutely! We offer volume discounts for large orders. Contact us for a custom quote based on your quantity and specifications.
